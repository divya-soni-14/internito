Why interNito?

Internships are hard. Their interview processes are even harder. And it is not that you get to redo them if something goes wrong! So how do you go about something that you've never done before, and yet ace it like a pro? Simple. You take advice from those who have already done it before.

It is with this cause in mind, that the HTML and DJango files of interNito were brought into existence. We're taught programming so as to solve a lot of assignments, competitive problems and God knows what not. But, ultimately, it is the real world problems that need solving. Internships were such a real world problem, and interNito is our proposed solution.

If you're a senior who's already completed the internship process, then this platform is your opportunity to help your juniors out! All the preparation that worked (and all that didn't!), all the OT challenges that you skillfully faced, and all the interview questions that you aced - you can share them all for the upcoming batches to benefit from!

And if you're a junior, you don't need to look anywhere else to solve your interview dilemmas! What to do during the interview? What kind of questions they ask? How to sit? How to talk? How to breath?? The website is your one stop to answer all of these questions, are more! P.S. for the last one, we'd say, just be natural!
